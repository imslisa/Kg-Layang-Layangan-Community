Uncover the Wonders of Kg Layang-Layangan


ABOUT


Kg Layang-Layangan is one of 27 villages exist in Federal Territory of Labuan.

The named of the village come from the small island located in front of the village that was

around 80 square meters which used to have many types of snakes and birds named

'Layang-Layang'. The village is strategically located and gifted with a beautiful

and mesmerizing view of the ocean.


BRIEF HISTORY


Kg Layang-Layangan also popular due to its historical attractions.

There is park called Peace Park that serves as a World War II memorial and poignant

reminder of the horrors of war. It is been stated in history that Labuan was under Japanese

occupation for four year before being yielded to Allied Forces on 10th September 1945.

This park was built by the Japanese as a symbol of peace, harmony, and friendship as well

as a tribute to those who lost their lives during the World War II.
